The Story

Where it all began

I started my journey in 2022, right as the world was navigating the tail end of the pandemic. While physical marketing was at a standstill, I saw the untapped potential of digital ecosystems.

My first challenge was scaling a small business from the ground up during a time of global uncertainty. By focusing on community-driven content and organic strategy, I grew their following to 7,000+ and successfully converted 80% of that audience into recurring monthly clients — all without spending a single cent on ads.

That success caught the attention of my next major partner: a local school. What began as a social media project evolved into a role as their All-In-One Marketing Service Provider. I've spent the last few years defining their brand identity, hitting enrollment KPIs, and managing every creative touchpoint — from physical stationery to digital funnels.

The Evolution

Beyond marketing —
Automation

Experience has taught me that great marketing is only half the battle. To prevent lead churn and ensure no opportunity is wasted, I expanded into Marketing Automation.

By integrating tools like n8n, Zapier, and GoHighLevel, I bridge the gap between "getting a lead" and "securing a client." I don't just post content — I build conversion funnels that work while you sleep.
